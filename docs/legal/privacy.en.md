# Macfyi Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

This policy explains how Macfyi collects, uses, stores, and protects data.

## 1) Core Principles

We follow a **privacy-first** approach:
- Collect as little data as reasonably necessary.
- Process data locally on device whenever possible.
- Be transparent about what data is processed and which third parties are involved.

## 2) Data We May Process

Depending on features you use, we may process:

1. **Account and license data**  
   Example: login email, license status, and limited device activation metadata.

2. **Technical device and app data**  
   Example: app version, error logs, minimal performance telemetry, and limited device fingerprinting for security/rate limiting.

3. **AI feature data**  
   Data sent for AI inference is limited to required context.  
   By product design, sensitive full file paths are intended not to be sent to external providers.

4. **Transaction/payment data**  
   Payment data is handled by the relevant payment gateway providers. We do not store full payment card details.

## 3) Purposes of Processing

We process data to:
- Deliver core app functionality.
- Operate authentication and license activation.
- Maintain service security (including abuse/rate-limit controls).
- Provide technical support.
- Improve product stability and quality.

## 4) Legal Bases

Processing relies on one or more of:
- Performance of a contract with users.
- Compliance with legal obligations.
- Legitimate interests that are proportionate (such as service security).
- User consent, where required by applicable law.

## 5) Local vs Server Processing

- Certain data is processed locally on user devices.
- Data required for service synchronization (account, licensing, cloud features) is processed on servers.
- We aim to minimize data sent off-device.

## 6) Third-Party Service Providers

We may use third-party providers, including:
- Backend/database infrastructure (for example, Supabase).
- AI model providers (for example, Gemini, Groq) for specific AI features.
- Payment providers (according to active checkout gateway).

These providers process data under their own policies and applicable agreements.

## 7) Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for legitimate purposes, including:
- While accounts/licenses remain active.
- For legal compliance, audits, or dispute handling.
- Technical logs for limited operational periods.

When data is no longer required, we delete or anonymize it where feasible.

## 8) Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or deletion.

No transmission or storage method is fully secure.

## 9) Cross-Border Transfers

If infrastructure or providers operate in other jurisdictions, data may be processed across borders. We seek to use reasonable and proportionate safeguards.

## 10) User Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request:
- Access to personal data.
- Correction of inaccurate data.
- Deletion of certain data.
- Restriction of or objection to certain processing.
- Withdrawal of consent (for consent-based processing).

Requests can be submitted through our privacy contact channel.

## 11) Children's Privacy

The services are not intended for children below the age threshold defined by local law without parent/guardian consent. If we identify unlawfully collected child data, we will take reasonable deletion steps.

## 12) Policy Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through reasonable channels. The "Last updated" date will be revised accordingly.

- Support email: **support@macfyi.com**
